Good to know

Before you go

Everything a first-time foam family asks. Got another question? Reach out and we'll take care of you.

Who is it for?+

Everyone. Foam Nation days are all-ages and built for families — little kids, big kids, teens, parents and grandparents all welcome. We also run 18+ nights after dark; watch this page for dates.

What should we wear & bring?+

Come in a swimsuit or clothes you don't mind getting foamy. Bring a towel, a change of clothes, water and sunscreen — it's an Arizona afternoon. Water shoes or sandals are a good call.

Where is it?+

Dink & Dine Pickle Park in Mesa, AZ — a family experience venue with food, drinks and pickleball on site. Tap "Get directions" in the Venue section for the map.

Is the foam safe?+

Yes — we use Foam Daddy's professional foam solution: hypoallergenic, non-toxic, biodegradable and non-staining. It's gentle on kids' skin and rinses clean off skin and swimsuits. If anyone has specific sensitivities, just reach out and we'll take care of you.

How do tickets work?+

All tickets are sold securely through Posh — tap any "Get tickets" button to check out right here on the site. Prices rise as each date gets closer, so early bird is the move.

What if it's my first foam party?+

You're in great hands. Foam Nation is built by the DartyForLife team behind some of the biggest events in Arizona, with 13,000+ guests hosted across the Valley. Just show up ready to play.
